My name is Qiaochu (Michelle) Zhang, who is currently pursuing a graduate degree in Computer Science at Northeastern University. I previously studied Information System Management in the business school and gained a year of experience as a software implementation engineer.


With a dedicated focus on web and mobile technologies, I've accumulated valuable project experience. In early this year, I utilized Flask to establish this personal blog, aiming to document and share insights from both my personal and academic journey.

I am a food enthusiast who not only loves savoring delicious dishes but also enjoys the art of cooking. Over the next year, I'll be sharing the culinary delights I discover and the dishes I create, categorizing these posts as "Foods."

My journey of exploration extends beyond the kitchen— I never cease to explore the world. In travel-related articles, I'll provide travel guides and share my experiences and observations.
